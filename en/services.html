Transportation

Airport Transportation

Airport MRT: Direct to Taipei Main Station, about 35 minutes

Airport Bus: Connects various districts in Taoyuan, frequent service

Taxi: 24-hour service, can be booked in advance

Car Rental: Rental counters available inside the airport

City Transportation

Taoyuan MRT: Connects the airport and the city

Bus System: Dense routes, EasyCard accepted

YouBike: Public bicycles, suitable for short trips

Taxi: Hail on the street, supports ride-hailing apps

Tourism Transportation

Lala Mountain Shuttle: Departs from Daxi, multiple trips daily

Shimen Reservoir Lake Tour Boat: Lakeside sightseeing, scheduled departures

Daxi Old Street Shuttle: Free shuttle connecting parking lots and old street

Charter Service: One-day tour charter available

Migrant Workers

Taoyuan is an important gathering place for foreign migrant workers, providing various services and support.
