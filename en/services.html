Transportation

Airport Transportation

Airport MRT: Direct to Taipei Main Station, about 35 minutes

Airport Bus: Connects various districts in Taoyuan, frequent service

Taxi: 24-hour service, can be booked in advance

Car Rental: Rental counters available inside the airport

City Transportation

Taoyuan MRT: Connects the airport and the city

Bus System: Dense routes, EasyCard accepted

YouBike: Public bicycles, suitable for short trips

Taxi: Hail on the street, supports ride-hailing apps

Tourism Transportation

Lala Mountain Shuttle: Departs from Daxi, multiple trips daily

Shimen Reservoir Lake Tour Boat: Lakeside sightseeing, scheduled departures

Daxi Old Street Shuttle: Free shuttle connecting parking lots and old street

Charter Service: One-day tour charter available

Migrant Workers

Taoyuan is an important gathering place for foreign migrant workers, providing various services and support.

Language Services

Multilingual Consultation: Provides services in Thai, Vietnamese, Indonesian, and Filipino

Translation Services: Translation services in medical, legal, and employment settings

Language Learning: Chinese courses and cultural adaptation courses

Contact: New Immigrants Family Service Center

Legal Assistance

Labor Rights: Consultation on working conditions, salary, working hours, etc

Legal Consultation: Free legal consultation services

Complaint Channels: Ministry of Labor and Immigration Department complaint hotlines

Emergency Assistance: 24-hour emergency hotline

Medical Services

Multilingual Healthcare: Hospitals providing multilingual healthcare services

Health Checkups: Regular health checkup services

Mental Health Counseling: Mental health support services

Emergency Medical Care: 24-hour emergency services

Life Support

Accommodation Assistance: Dormitory management, rental information

Financial Services: Account opening, remittance, insurance services

Shopping Guide: Purchasing local products and daily necessities

Festival and Events: Festivals and celebrations from various countries

Tourism

Tourist Information Center

Taoyuan Railway Station: Provides maps, attraction information, and transportation information

Taoyuan International Airport: 24-hour service with multilingual support

Daxi Old Street: Local culture tours and food recommendations

Lala Mountain: Hiking information, weather forecasts, and safety tips

Guided Tours

Professional Guides: Multilingual guided tours

Cultural Tours: Hakka culture, Yunnan-Burma culture tours

Nature Tours: Lala Mountain, Shimen Reservoir ecological tours

Food Tours: Local cuisine, international cuisine tours
