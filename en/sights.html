Featured Attractions in Taoyuan

Natural Scenery

Lalashan (Mt. Lala)

Altitude: 1,500-2,000 meters

Sea of clouds, maple leaves, and giant cypress tree trails, offering scenic views throughout the four seasons. Cherry blossoms in spring, a summer retreat, maple leaves in autumn, and a magnificent sea of clouds in winter.

Highlights: Divine Tree Cluster, Sea of Clouds, Cherry Blossom Season

Recommended Stay: Half day to full day

Xiao Wulai Scenic Area

Highlights: Skywalk, Waterfall, Rainbow Bridge

Known for its Skywalk and spectacular waterfall, offering views of the valley and Rainbow Bridge. The 11-meter-long Skywalk features a transparent glass floor for a thrilling experience.

Opening Hours: 08:00-17:00

Admission: Full price NT$50

Shihmen Reservoir (Shihmen Dam)

Highlights: Lake Cycling Path, Sunset Views, Fresh Fish Cuisine

The 15 km cycling path circles the lake, allowing visitors to enjoy the beautiful water and mountain scenery. The dam crest is an excellent spot for watching the sunset, and many fresh fish restaurants are nearby.

Suggested Activities: Cycling, Sunset Viewing, Tasting Fresh Fish

Best Time: 3 PM - 6 PM

Cultural Heritage

Daxi Old Street

Highlights: Baroque Shopfronts, Wood Craft Culture, Old House Cafés

Well-preserved Baroque facades and wood art culture make this a great place for a nostalgic walk. The streets are lined with traditional dried tofu shops, wood craft stores, and coffee shops.

Must-Eat: Daxi Dried Tofu, Peanut Candy

Must-Buy: Wood Crafts, Traditional Pastries

Zhongzhen New Village

Highlights: Burmese-Yunnan Flavor Settlement, Yunnan Cuisine

A settlement of immigrants from Myanmar and Yunnan, the streets are filled with Yunnan cuisine and historical stories. This is the best place to savor authentic Yunnan dishes.

Must-Eat: Layered Steamed Buns (Po Su Bao), Rice Noodles (Mi Gan), Cold Papaya Salad

Culture: Historical Exhibition of Burmese-Yunnan Immigrants

Transportation Landmarks

Taoyuan International Airport (TPE)

Highlights: Gateway to Taiwan, Modern Facilities

Taiwan's international gateway, connecting the world with the local area. The airport offers extensive shopping and dining options, serving as the first stop for experiencing Taiwanese culture.

Facilities: Duty-Free Shops, Food Courts, Lounges

Transportation: Airport MRT Direct to Taipei

Cultural Villages

Hakka Tea Culture Museum

Highlights: Hakka and International Tea Culture

A new hub of Hakka tea culture that blends culture, history, and cuisine, suitable for all travelers to stop and savor.

Experiences: Hakka Lei Cha (Pound Tea), Handmade Floral/Fruit Tea

KIRI International Indigenous Cultural and Creative Park

Highlights: Indigenous-Style Products

The shops are planned around four main themes: Cultural & Creative, Trendy, Gourmet, and Lifestyle.

Experiences: Art Tattoo Painting, Boar Tusk Earrings, Specialty Cuisine

Recommended Travel Routes

Mountain One-Day Trip

Route: Lalashan → Xiao Wulai → Daxi Old Street

Morning visit to Lalashan to see the Divine Tree cluster and sea of clouds, afternoon trip to Xiao Wulai for the Skywalk, and evening enjoying traditional food at Daxi Old Street.

Suggested Duration: 8-10 hours

Transportation: Recommended self-drive or chartered car

Reservoir Cycling Tour

Route: Shihmen Reservoir → Lake Cycling Path → Fresh Fish Restaurant

Morning cycling around the lake to enjoy the scenery, lunch savoring fresh fish cuisine, and afternoon watching the sunset from the dam crest.

Suggested Duration: 6-8 hours

Suitable for: Families, Couples

Old Street Cultural Tour

Route: Daxi Old Street → Zhongzhen New Village → Hakka Cultural Village

Experience the characteristics of different cultural settlements, from Baroque architecture to Burmese-Yunnan flavors, and Hakka culture.

Suggested Duration: 4-6 hours

Highlights: Cultural Experience, Food Exploration

Airport Area Tour

Route: Taoyuan International Airport → Gloria Outlets → Qingpu HSR Station

Ideal for transit passengers making a brief stop, experiencing Taiwanese shopping and dining culture.

Suggested Duration: 2-4 hours

Transportation: Airport MRT
