What is "Little Southeast Asia"?

Taoyuan has one of Taiwan's largest industrial clusters, where migrant workers from Thailand, Vietnam, Indonesia, Philippines and other countries work and live, creating unique multilingual dining streets and festival activities.

Exotic Flavors of Zhongzhen New Village

Yunnan cuisine brought by Yunnan-Burma immigrants has taken root here, with sour and spicy appetizing flavors and herbs in dishes, making it an unmissable local taste.
